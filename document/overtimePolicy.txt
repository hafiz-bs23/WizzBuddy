Overtime Policy of Brain Station 23 is as follows:
1. Only first line supervisor of individual employee can raise OT requests for his subordinates.
2. Minimum OT eligible hour is 1hr (One hour) or multiples of 1hr (one hour). No fraction is applicable.
3. Approval by HR Manager is needed for the final confirmation of any OT request.
4. The excess time of planned hour will be considered as OT working hours. i.e. There must be a attendance plan for OT eligibility.
5. OT calculation: (Gross Salary/ 160) * Approved OT hour (but not more than the actual OT hour)
